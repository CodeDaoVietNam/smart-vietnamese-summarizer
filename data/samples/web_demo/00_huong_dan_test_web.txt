- Lecture/study material: study_notes nen co Khai niem chinh, Can nho, Vi du, Loi de nham.
- Article: concise nen la mot doan ngan tu nhien, bullet nen la danh sach y chinh.
- Case 02 khong co task ro rang, action_items ly tuong nen noi khong co viec can lam ro rang.
- Diem Heuristic Quality Estimate chi la proxy dua tren do dai, lap tu, keyword coverage va tin hieu generation; khong phai xac suat dung sai.
